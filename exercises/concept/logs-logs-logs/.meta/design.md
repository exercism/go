# Design

## Goal

The goal of this exercise is to teach the student about runes.

## Learning objectives

- Understand the `rune` type in Go.
- Understand how runes relate to Unicode and UTF-8.
- Understand that runes can be 1, 2, 3, or 4 bytes.
